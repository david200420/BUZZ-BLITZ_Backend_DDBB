Who are we?

In "Buzz Blitz", you dive into the thrilling journey of a lost bee who must find her way back to the hive — now under threat from a cunning spider.

Our team is dedicated to delivering a unique experience by blending the nostalgic charm of classic Crossy Road-style gameplay with a fresh, original theme full of surprises.

Explore a vibrant world filled with flowers, honey-filled levels, and reflex-testing challenges. Collect powerful swords, unlock exclusive skins, and prepare for the ultimate showdown against the spider boss in the final stage.

Join the adventure and help save the hive! Whether you're here for the fast-paced action or the buzzing atmosphere, Buzz Blitz is the game for you.
